Why we chose this model

The security industry runs on fear-rent — selling protection at the price the market will bear when someone is panicking about a breach. We don't want to be that. Vibe-coders are exactly the audience priced out of every existing security tool, and they're the audience most likely to ship a bug that hurts real users.

So Lictor's core stays free, forever. Voluntary support from the people it helps keeps the maintainer fed. A small commercial tier (Lictor for Teams) funds the rest — for organizations that want continuous monitoring and audit-log export. The free tier already does everything a single builder needs.

— Raffa
